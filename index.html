You Are Welcome
You are welcome to Independence chorale, FCT, Abuja, a Christian based chorale group that aimed at inspiring it community through artistic music and innovation in chorale performances and educative programmes.

It all started January 1st, 2022. And this initial idea brought us to our first concert Titled "The Complete Handel's Messiah" in October 3rd, 2022. In honour of the Name independence chorale

As it is we know that the sky is only our beginning. We are not perfect but we always put in our best to achieve greater heights. We have been achieving this by his grace through personal, collective, professional and mutual endeavors. And all this we bring into focus to realise our vision and mission.
